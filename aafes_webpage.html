About Our School

Al Amir Foundation Education System is committed to excellence in education. Our principal, Mubina Shaheen, along with Class 9th teacher Sir Shahid Rasool, ensures a learning environment where every student achieves their full potential.

We focus on holistic development, providing academic, moral, and social education.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in life. Our vision is to be a beacon of excellence in education, nurturing future leaders.

We strive to create a supportive environment that encourages students to explore their interests and talents.

Our Facilities

We offer state-of-the-art facilities including:

  • Modern science labs equipped for practical experiments
  • A comprehensive library with a vast collection of books
  • Spacious sports grounds for various activities
  • Technology-enabled classrooms for interactive learning

Student Life

Our students enjoy a vibrant campus life with numerous extracurricular activities, including:

  • Sports teams for football, basketball, and cricket
  • Art and music programs to nurture creativity
  • Debate and drama clubs to enhance communication skills
  • Leadership opportunities through student council

Our Achievements

We are proud of our numerous academic and extracurricular achievements:

  • Top rankings in regional and national exams
  • Winners of several inter-school competitions
  • Outstanding performances in sports and arts
